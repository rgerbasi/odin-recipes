Quick Gnocchi


Description

Easier version of the Italian favorite fluffy pasta pillows.

Ingredients

  • Dry potato flakes
  • 1 Egg
  • Salt and pepper
  • Flour

Steps

  1. Place potato flakes in medium size bowl, pour boiling water and stir until blended.
  2. Sit in egg, salt, and pepper
  3. Turn dough out on a well floured board and knead lightly
  4. Divide dough in half and shape each half into a long thin roll.
  5. Dip the knife in flour and cut into bite sized pieces
  6. Boil the gnocchi in water and as they rise remove them.

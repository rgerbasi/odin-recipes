Marry Me Chicken Soup


Description

The delicious sauteed chicken that is usually over pasta but now over some soup

Ingredients

  • Sun-dried tomatoes
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Tomato Paste
  • Chicken Broth
  • Heavy Cream
  • Pasta
  • Chicken
  • Basil
  • Red pepper flakes
  • Spinach
  • Cream Cheese
  • Parmesan

Steps

  1. Sautee onion and garlic in the oil from the tomatoes
  2. Add tomatoes and tomato paste
  3. Pour in broth, cream, and seasonings
  4. Simmer and then add the dried pasta
  5. Add spinach, chicken, cream cheese, and parmesan while stirring
  6. Garnish

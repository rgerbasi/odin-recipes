Marry Me Chicken Soup


Description

Ingredients

  • Sweetened condensed milk
  • Unsweetened cocoa
  • Butter
  • Chocolate sprinkles

Steps

  1. Combine condensed milk, cocoa, and butter in a medium saucepan over medium heat. Cook and stir for about 10 minutes
  2. Transfer to shallow bowl and cool
  3. Pinch some of the dough and roll into small ball
  4. Roll in chocolate sprinkles
  5. Enjoy
